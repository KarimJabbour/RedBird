About RedBird Roster

RedBird Roster is McGill's meeting and office hour management system for professors, students and TAs. Schedule office hours, book meetings and send polls all from one platform.

Easy Scheduling

Easily setup your office hours and share a URL with your students.

Reschedule

Change of plans? You can edit your meetings and RedBird will automatically email all the attendees to let them know of the changes so you can reschedule without the hassle.

Planning

Setup polls to vote for the best time to hold a meeting. Attendees can select their available times so you can minimize scheduling issues.
